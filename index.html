My Story

I have been a stay-at-home-mom for the last 10+ years. A couple of years ago, I discovered coding through the Hour of Code, Code.Org's initiative to get every student across the U.S. to try one hour of code. For the last few years, I have been learning JavaScript, HTML, and CSS in my spare time.

When I learned about the Galvanize Web Development Program, I applied, got accepted, and made the decision to leave my husband and four boys in Lawrence, Kansas for half a year and go to Denver to learn the Full Stack.

I spent six months building web applications for 40-60 hours a week. Rooted in the NEAP stack (Node/Express/Angular/PostgreSQL), our curriculum at Galvanize was focused on JavaScript fundamentals.

Most recently, I was working as a Content Developer for AMC Theatres. I am currently looking for a role as a Junior Developer with a company that fosters growth where I can continue to learn!
